Trimming the space between full-width punctuation and full-width character
In Japanese, the space between punctuation and character in full-width can be specified by -ah-punctuation-spacing. The specified space is applied between the full-width closing parenthesis and opening parenthesis, between the full-width closing parenthesis and full-width non-punctuation, between the full-width non-punctuation and full-width opening parenthesis when -ah-punctuation-trim: adjacent is specified. The value can be specified by the absolute or percentage. The default value of the space to trim can be specified by -ah-punctuation-spacing in the option setting file. The default value of is -ah-punctuation-spacing: 50%.
-ah-punctuation-trim: adjacent, without the setting of -ah-punctuation-spacing
（あい「うえお」「「かきく」けこ」）
-ah-punctuation-trim: adjacent, without the setting of -ah-punctuation-spacing: 1em
（あい「うえお」「「かきく」けこ」）
-ah-punctuation-trim: adjacent, without the setting of -ah-punctuation-spacing: 2em
（あい「うえお」「「かきく」けこ」）
-ah-punctuation-trim: adjacent, without the setting of -ah-punctuation-spacing: 0%
（あい「うえお」「「かきく」けこ」）
-ah-punctuation-trim: adjacent, without the setting of -ah-punctuation-spacing: 50%
（あい「うえお」「「かきく」けこ」）
-ah-punctuation-trim: adjacent, without the setting of -ah-punctuation-spacing: 200%
（あい「うえお」「「かきく」けこ」）
