Trimming text at the end of line
Specify whether to trim the text or not. If none is specified to -ah-text-justify-trim, the text is not trimmed. Japanese full-width parentheses, middle dots, and punctuations are also trimmed by specifying punctuation. Spaces between Hiragana and Katakana are trimmed by specifying kana, spaces between Kanji and Kana are trimmed by specifying ideograph and spaces between Western words can also be trimmed by specifying inter-word.
