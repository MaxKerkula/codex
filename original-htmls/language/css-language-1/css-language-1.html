Multiple languages
Antenna House Formatter supports formatting Latin characters plus Cyrillic, Greek, Japanese, Simplified Chinese, Traditional Chinese, Hangul, Arabic, Hebrew, Devanagari, Thai and most characters that can be represented in Unicode. Antenna House Formatter supports more than 80 languages. In addition, Burmese (Myanmar) is supported by AH Formatter V6.3 and later. Ethiopic and Syriac are supported by AH Formatter V6.6 and later. Armenian and Georgian are supported by AH Formatter V7.1 and later.
The samples use an excerpt from UDHR in Unicode. Telugu, Oriya, Sinhala, Ethiopic, Syriac samples are excerpted from Wikipedia
