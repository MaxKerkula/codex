Line numbers to always show
‘-ah-line-number-show’ specifies line numbers that will always be shown. The specified numbers are generated in addition to numbers generated by ‘-ah-line-number-interval’, etc. For more details on ‘-ah-line-number-show’, please refer to the Online Manual. ‘-ah-line-number-show’ is available in AH Formatter V6.5 and later.
When -ah-line-number-interval="10" is specified, the line number is displayed every 10 lines, as in the left column. In the right column, -ah-line-number-show="1" is specified in addition to -ah-line-number-interval="10", and the line number for line 1 is also shown.
