Generating Wavy Borders

The border of border-style: wave; can be specified like -ah-border-wave-form: 10 3pt;. One or two values may be specified. The values in order from left to right are wave form length and line width. Each value may be a number, a length, or 'auto'. Numeric values are proportional to the border width. If a value is 'auto' or omitted, it is regarded as either 6 or 0.125, respectively. Negative values are not available. border-style: wave; is available in AH Formatter V6.3 and later.
border-style: wave;
border-style: wave; -ah-border-wave-form: auto;
border-style: wave; -ah-border-wave-form: 6 0.125;
border-style: wave; -ah-border-wave-form: 2;
border-style: wave; -ah-border-wave-form: 5;
border-style: wave; -ah-border-wave-form: 10;
border-style: wave; -ah-border-wave-form: 10 3pt;
border-style: wave; -ah-border-wave-form: 10 5pt;
