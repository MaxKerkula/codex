Page breaks at even or odd pages from the top of the document
By specifying break-after and break-before with ‘even-document’ or ‘odd-document’ will cause page breaks on even or odd pages counting from the top of the document. For more details on break-after break-before, please refer to the Online Manual. This feature is available with Antenna House Formatter V7.3 or later.
